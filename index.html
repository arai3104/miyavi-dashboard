J-WID Source Type Glossary

The source type (出典) indicates how and where a work was registered in J-WID. Codes appear in the work detail header as e.g. 外国作品■出典：PJ(サブ出版者作品届).

Code Japanese Name Meaning In dataset
PJサブ出版者作品届Sub-publisher work registration. Foreign work registered in Japan by a local sub-publisher (e.g. Universal Music Publishing Japan).126
PO出版者作品届（洋楽）Publisher work registration, overseas/other. Foreign work registered directly by an overseas publisher without a Japanese sub-publisher.104
PW出版者作品届（洋楽標準）Standard Western/foreign work registration. Typically used for works from Western PRO repertoire (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, PRS, etc.).14
SEサブ出版者届（英語）Sub-publisher entry, English-language repertoire. Similar to PJ but for English-language works with a specific sub-publishing arrangement.10
PE出版者作品届（電子）Electronic/digital work registration. Domestic or foreign works registered primarily for digital distribution (streaming, downloads).6
SVサブ出版者届（映像）Sub-publisher entry, video/visual works. Used for works tied to video productions (music video, TV use) with a Japanese sub-publisher.4
SMサブ出版者届（音楽）Sub-publisher entry, music category. General music works registered through a sub-publishing deal.1
PD出版者作品届（ドラマ）Publisher registration, drama/tie-up. Works specifically registered in connection with a TV drama or other theatrical tie-up.1
